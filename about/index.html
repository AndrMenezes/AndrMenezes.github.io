About

I am statistician with experience in statistical modeling and data analysis.

I am currently a Ph.D. Student in Data Science at Maynooth University funded by SFI Centre for Research Training in Foundations of Data Science.

My academic background is:

  • (Sep 2022 – Ongoing) Ph.D. in Data Science, Maynooth University.

  • (Mar 2019 – May 2021) M.Sc. in Statistics, State Univesity of Campinas. Advised by professor Benilton de Sá Carvalho I studied statistical methods for scRNA-seq data modeling.

  • (Feb 2014 – Feb 2019) B.Sc. in Statistics, State University of Maringá. Advised by professor Josmar Mazucheli, I studied bias correction methods of maximum likelihood estimators and probability distributions with support on the unit interval (0, 1).

Besides research, I was an intern during seven months (Ago/2018 – Feb/2019) in credit department of Bradesco bank.

Furthermore, I have worked as Data Scientist at Murabei Data Science for 1.5 years (Mar/2019 – Jul/2022).
