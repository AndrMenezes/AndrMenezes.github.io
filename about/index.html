About

I’m statistician with experience in statistical modeling and data analysis. My background academic is:

  • (2019 – 2021) M.Sc Statistics, State Univesity of Campinas. Advised by professor Benilton de Sá Carvalho I studied statistical methods for scRNA-seq data modeling.

  • (2014 – 2018) B.Sc. Statistics, State University of Maringá. Advised by professor Josmar Mazucheli, I studied bias correction methods of maximum likelihood estimators and probability distributions with support on the unit interval (0, 1).

I was an intern during seven months (ago/2018 – feb/2019) in credit department of Bradesco bank.

I currently work as a data scientist at Murabei Data Science.
